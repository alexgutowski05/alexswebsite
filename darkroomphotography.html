Darkroom Photography

This was technically my second time taking this class, however, the first time was during a pandemic, so I didn't get to use the darkroom. I was able to retake this class in junior year and I loved working in the darkroom. I learned how to use an enlarger, how to develop negatives, how to develop actual images, and how to use a film camera. I had a lot of fun with this class, and I will keep using photography later in life, whether that is through a profession, or just as a hobby.
