In my senior year, in the effort to take every single class taught by Mr. Bush, I decided to take Digital Photography. In Digital Photography we learn about photography cameras, photography composition types, how to edit photos using Adobe Lightroom and Adobe Photoshop, and tools to be a better photographer.

I enjoy photography because it's a way of expressing my creativity. I'm not super great at drawing, painting, or "traditional" art forms, so I feel like the only way for me to express that creativity is through digital media. I've been taking digital media classes since seventh grade, and I have loved every minute of it.

Check out some of my work below. Use the arrows at the right and left of each image to flip through the different photos for the different assignments.
