"Digital" Photography

In freshman year, I was supposed to take a class in Darkroom Photography. However, the COVID-19 pandemic made that difficult. How are you supposed to use a darkroom in a virtual setting? Luckly, my teacher came up with an alternate solution. The class turned into a digital photography class, except we used Adobe Lightroom to make the images black and white after we shot them. It was less than ideal, but it was the best we could do for virtual. I was luckly to be able to take the class again and actually use the darkroom in junior year.

Check out some of my work below. Use the arrows at the right and left of each image to flip through the different photos for the different assignments.
