SHS Today


I was lucky enough to be invited to Video News Production as a sophomore, when the class is usually meant for juniors and seniors only. I learned a lot about creating preproduced media (packages), creating live productions, and working together in a team to create a final project. I am very proud to be a part of a group that managed to win a gold award from the Michigan Interscholastic Press Assosiation (MIPA) and a Student Excellence Award from the National Academy of Television Arts and Sciences (NATAS). I myself also won third place in Localized News Analysis for my package on Autonomous Cars.

I was chosen to be one of two producers for the show for the 2021-2022 season, and out first show was a great sucess! I've learned a lot about managing a team, and I can't wait to see what my team does later in the year.
