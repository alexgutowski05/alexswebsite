Welcome to my portfolio!


Hello! My name is Alex Gutowski. I'm a current Senior at Saline High School in Saline, MI and I'm committed to Michigan State University for the 2023 Fall Semester. I've taken many digital arts classes and produced countless pieces of work. I'm a producer for SHS Today, one of the two student news shows, as well as a technical director for the Saline Live Video Crew, a team of people who livestream sports, shows in the theater, and other events around the district. Both of those positions are the highest position offered to a student in their respective field. Outside of broadcast, I've also taken numerous photography and graphic design classes and I enjoy taking photos in my everyday life.

Outside the world of digital media, I'm a member of Hornet Light and Sound, an extracurricular club that runs tech in our district's theater spaces. Our drama club puts on two shows every year in our theater that we run tech for, along with district music concerts, board meetings, and we provide tech to people who rent out the space, who utilize it for plays, musicals, dance recitals, and awards ceremonies. In this club, I serve the role of Junior Supervisor which, like the programs listed above, is also the highest role offered to a student.
