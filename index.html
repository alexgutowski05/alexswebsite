Welcome to my portfolio!

Hello! My name is Alex Gutowski. I’m a current Sophomore at Michigan State University majoring in Digital Storytelling. I’m currently the Tech Director of MSU Telecasters, working with all the shows in our network to manage their technology requirements.

Before I went to Michigan State, I was enrolled at Saline High School. There I took many digital arts classes and produced countless pieces of work. I was a producer for SHS Today, one of two student news shows our school produces, as well as a technical director for the Saline Live Video Crew, a team of people who livestream sports, shows in the theater, and other events around the district. Both positions were the highest positions offered to a student in their respective field. Outside of broadcast, I also took numerous photography and graphic design classes and I enjoy taking photos in my everyday life.
