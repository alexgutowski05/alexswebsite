Videography is what I spend most of my time on. Our broadcast program at our high school was one of my favorite classes. The studio where we shoot our show was my one of my favorite places in the school. I worked on the Saline Live Video Crew, live streaming events across the district to people all over the country. Videography is what I love.

In high school, I was invited to take SHS Today, our school's student news program, in my sophomore year. This class is usually reserved for Juniors and Seniors, but the advisor, Mr. Bush, invited me after I took his Video Production 1 class as a freshman. In SHS Today, I learned everything I know about broadcast media and produced some award-winning content that you can see below. In both my Junior and Senior year in the program, I was a producer managing a team of 7-10 people. Throughout this program, I've created lots of video projects I'm proud of, made lifelong friends, and developed a place where I can express myself freely. I'm proud to be a part of this program.
